How It Works

Three steps. That's all.

Open a browser on both devices. No downloads. No logins.

01
📁

Drop your files

Open OffiqShare on your device. Drop any files — images, videos, folders, ZIPs, anything. No file type restrictions, no size limit.

02
🔗

Share 3 ways

A QR code, a 6-letter code, and a shareable link are all generated instantly. Send via WhatsApp, SMS, or just show the screen to scan.

03
✅

Receiver accepts

Receiver opens OffiqShare on any device, sees a preview of the files, and taps Accept. The direct P2P transfer begins immediately.

04
⚡

Files arrive

Direct device-to-device at full speed. Files are encrypted end-to-end — they never touch our server. Receiver saves them with one tap.

Why OffiqShare

Built different. Works everywhere.

∞

No size limit — ever

Files go peer-to-peer, never through our servers. Send a 100 GB video just like a 10 KB document.

🔒

End-to-end encrypted

WebRTC uses DTLS-SRTP encryption. Files are encrypted in transit and never stored anywhere.

📱

Any device, any OS

Android ↔ iOS ↔ macOS ↔ Windows ↔ Linux. Chrome, Safari, Firefox — all supported.

3️⃣

3 ways to connect

QR code scan, 6-letter code, or shareable link. Pick what's easiest — all three are generated automatically.

✅

Receiver approval

Receiver sees a preview of incoming files and explicitly accepts before any transfer begins. Full control.

🌐

No account required

Open the page, share, done. Zero sign-ups, zero tracking, zero nonsense. Forever.

🔄

Resume Interrupted Transfers

Connection dropped mid-transfer? OffiqShare preserves progress and gives you 30 minutes to reconnect and resume exactly where it left off.

🕓

Transfer History

Every completed, failed, or cancelled transfer is logged locally. Review past sessions, see file names and sizes, and clear history any time.
